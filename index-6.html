Two Rivers Hotel

Indulge in the Experience

We are nestled in Kazungula, the heart of the Chobe region—where four of Southern Africa’s premier safari destinations converge: Botswana, Zambia, Zimbabwe, and Namibia.
A sanctuary for both tranquility and adventure, our hotel draws its name and spirit from the majestic Chobe and Zambezi Rivers. These two mighty waterways meet near the iconic Kazungula Bridge, creating a natural crossroads between nations: Botswana and Namibia on the Chobe side, and Zambia and Zimbabwe along the Zambezi.
Here, where rivers and borders intertwine, Two Rivers Hotel offers a gateway to untamed wilderness and unforgettable experiences.

Land Adventures

Game Drive & Kazungula Bridge Tour

Embark on an unforgettable journey through untamed wilderness on a thrilling game drive, where elephants roam freely and predators rule the savanna. Then, witness the marvel of the Kazungula Bridge—where four nations meet and the mighty Zambezi and Chobe Rivers converge. A perfect blend of wild encounters and engineering wonder.

Water Escapes

Boat Cruise & Victoria Falls Experience

Glide along the tranquil waters of the Chobe or Zambezi River at sunset, sipping sundowners as hippos bask and elephants wade along the banks. Then, witness the awe-inspiring power of Victoria Falls—"The Smoke That Thunders"—where mist-kissed rainbows dance above one of Earth’s greatest natural wonders. A symphony of serenity and raw, untamed beauty.
